About

Hello, my name is Ryan Joseph Coughlin. I am a Graphic and Web design candidate at The Art Institute of California – San Francisco. Throughout my educational experience, I have gained knowledge and proficiency in many of Abode’s products such as Premiere, Prelude, Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and DreamWeaver.

Though I am only over a year into my graphic and web design career, I have been creative my whole life. When I’m not working on schoolwork, you will likely find me with a pencil or camera in my hand.
